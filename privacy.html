Privacy Policy

How FraudDMS Panel protects your data and ensures compliance

Data Processing & Privacy

FraudDMS processes data locally, ensuring GDPR, AML, KYC compliance. Audit logs included in beta.

Local Processing

All fraud detection and analysis happens on your infrastructure. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties. This ensures complete data sovereignty and compliance with strict financial regulations.

Data Encryption

All stored data is encrypted using SQLCipher with AES-256 encryption. Database files are protected with industry-standard encryption keys that never leave your secure environment.

Audit Logging

Comprehensive audit logs are included in the beta version, tracking all system activities, fraud detection decisions, and user actions. These logs help maintain compliance with regulatory requirements.

Compliance Standards

GDPR Compliance

FraudDMS Panel is designed to meet General Data Protection Regulation requirements. Since all processing happens locally, you maintain full control over personal data processing and can ensure compliance with data subject rights.

AML (Anti-Money Laundering)

The system includes built-in AML compliance features, helping detect suspicious transaction patterns and generate reports required for regulatory compliance in financial services.

KYC (Know Your Customer)

Integration capabilities support KYC verification processes while maintaining data privacy through local processing and secure data handling.

Beta Program Data

During the beta program, we may collect anonymized performance metrics and usage statistics to improve the system. No sensitive financial data or personal information is collected or transmitted.

What We Collect

  • System performance metrics (anonymized)
  • Error logs (no sensitive data)
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Feedback and improvement suggestions

What We Don't Collect

  • Transaction data
  • Personal information
  • Financial records
  • Customer data

Contact & Questions

For questions about this privacy policy or data handling practices, contact us at beta@frauddms.com.
